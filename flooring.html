Flooring Types We Install

Hardwood

Classic and timeless. Solid or engineered hardwood for living rooms, bedrooms, and hallways. We handle installation, sanding, and finishing.

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

Durable, waterproof, and great for families with kids or pets. Looks like wood, handles moisture better. Perfect for kitchens and basements.

Laminate

A budget-friendly option that's come a long way. Good for bedrooms, offices, and low-moisture areas.

Tile

Porcelain and ceramic tile for bathrooms, kitchens, entryways, and laundry rooms. We handle layout, cutting, and grouting.

Flooring FAQ

How much does flooring installation cost?

Flooring costs depend on material and square footage. Expect $3-8 per square foot for materials plus installation. We provide quotes based on your specific project.

How long does flooring installation take?

Most single-room installations take 1-2 days. A full main floor can take 3-5 days depending on prep work and material.

What flooring is best for basements?

Luxury vinyl plank is our top recommendation for basements. It's waterproof, durable, and comfortable underfoot.

What flooring is best for homes with pets?

LVP is ideal. It's scratch-resistant, waterproof, and easy to clean.
