What's Included

Every kitchen is different, but here's what a typical renovation with us can include:

  • Cabinet removal and installation (stock, semi-custom, or custom)
  • Countertop installation (quartz, granite, laminate, butcher block)
  • Backsplash tile installation
  • Flooring (tile, hardwood, vinyl plank)
  • Lighting upgrades (pot lights, pendants, under-cabinet lighting)
  • Plumbing updates (sink, faucet, dishwasher hookup)
  • Electrical updates (outlets, switches, dedicated circuits)
  • Layout changes (moving walls, adjusting workflow)
  • Painting and trim

We work with licensed electricians and plumbers to ensure everything is done to code.

How Long Does a Kitchen Renovation Take?

Most kitchen renovations take 3-6 weeks depending on scope. A cabinet refresh with new counters might be 2-3 weeks. A full gut renovation with layout changes can take 6-8 weeks.

We'll give you a realistic timeline upfront so you can plan around it.
