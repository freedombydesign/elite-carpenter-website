Your Bathroom Should Be Better Than "Fine"

If you're avoiding your main bath because the tile is cracked, the vanity is falling apart, or it just feels dark and cramped, it's time for an upgrade.

A good bathroom renovation isn't just about looks. It's about function. Storage that makes sense. A layout that doesn't have you bumping elbows. Finishes that hold up to daily use.

Elite Carpenter Home Renovations handles bathroom renovations for families across the Hamilton and Niagara region. We coordinate the plumbing, electrical, tile, and finishing so you get one team, one point of contact, and a bathroom you actually like using.

How Long Does a Bathroom Renovation Take?

A typical full bathroom renovation takes 2-4 weeks. Smaller updates like a vanity swap and new flooring can be done in under a week. We'll give you a realistic timeline based on your specific project.
