Meet Sheldon Carpenter

Yes, that's his real name. And yes, he runs a renovation company.

A Contractor You Can Actually Talk To

Elite Carpenter Home Renovation is owned and operated by Sheldon Carpenter, a Hamilton-area contractor with over two decades of experience in the construction industry.

Sheldon grew up in a family of tradespeople. His brothers are in construction. He's spent years on job sites, understanding how projects come together, what can go wrong, and what it takes to get things done right.

He started Elite Carpenter because he saw how frustrating the renovation process can be for homeowners. Too many contractors who don't call back. Too many projects that drag on. Too much confusion about who's showing up and when.

Elite Carpenter is the opposite of that.

When you work with us, you deal with Sheldon. Not a call center. Not a rotating cast of project managers. One person who knows your project, answers your calls, and makes sure things stay on track.
